The process

How a piece comes to life

Nothing ships until it's actually made. Here's what happens between your note and your doorstep.

01

You tell us your idea

A quick form, a photo for reference, or just a rough idea — we'll ask the right questions.

02

We sketch & quote

A mock-up and a firm price land in your inbox within two business days.

03

It's handcrafted

Your piece is made in our Portland workshop, start to finish, by one of three makers.

04

Wrapped & shipped

It arrives tagged and ready to gift, with tracking sent the moment it leaves the shelf.
